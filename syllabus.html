Value and Quality in Healthcare

Dr. Cameron B. Chiarot

Welcome. This course outlines the principal components of a value-based healthcare system, adopting a Dual-Lens Strategy to navigate the interface between public health mandates and private-sector innovations.

Driven by an applied Code-Along Philosophy utilizing R/RStudio, the curriculum equips upcoming leaders to transform complex healthcare datasets into evidence-based insights for health system resilience.
